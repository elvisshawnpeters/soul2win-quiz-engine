## SOP-04 Tamil Translation

=================================

Status:
Pending

Preferred Translation Style

- Use natural academic Tamil.

- When an English scientific term is widely used by
  NEET aspirants, retain it in parentheses on the
  first occurrence.

Examples

Syngamy
→ இணைவு (Syngamy)

Triple Fusion
→ மூவிணைவு (Triple Fusion)

Sporophyll
→ ஸ்போரோஃபில் (Sporophyll)

Xylem
→ சைலம் (Xylem)

Priority

Low

## SOP-02 Chemistry

=================================

For thermochemistry questions:

- Verify that the wording of the question
  (absorbed / released)
  matches the sign convention
  of the correct answer and explanation.

Positive ΔH → Energy absorbed (endothermic)

Negative ΔH → Energy released (exothermic)
